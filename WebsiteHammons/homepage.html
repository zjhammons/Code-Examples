Elliot ends holdout, inks new contract to become highest paid RB in NFL

Ezekiel Elliot has ended his holdout for a new contract and it was worth the wait. Elliot's new contract extension is a six-year, $90 million dollar extension. First reported by NFL Network's Ian Rapoport, the total contract is $103 million over eight years with $50 million guaranteed. Elliot surpasses Todd Gurley as the new highest paid RB in the NFL. Owner Jerry Jones commented saying, "Zeke has been arguably our best player".
